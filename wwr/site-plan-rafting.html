Overview

Purpose

White water rafting is a recreation that is not commonly found in many places in Africa. Many africans would want to experience water rafting but could not, because they are not given a chance. This is the case in my counry, Nigeria. For this reason, this site is to create awareness to africans especially Nigerians residing in and around the country, who would love to have experience in water rafting; that there is one now located in Nigeria with low and affordable charges rate, which will aim at creating sport.

Audience

According to research, there are many people who are scared of navigating on water. This site is capable of answering the following questions:
Who are our target customers?
These are interested Africans, especially Nigerians who are specifically youths between the age of 18 to 40 who are vibrant and specialize in sport, can take care of their finance and reside within and around the country.
What do they want?
These are people who may be new to rafting but really need this activity around them. They want to be guided to know how active we are, how we engage with people for this activity, our fixttures and so on.
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
There are questions available for our visitors to answer like:
What is your name?
Where are you from?
How old are you?
Are you a type who can engage in sport?
Can you take care of your needs?
What is your request?
What are your needs that aren't being met?
We are so much interested in your comment to see how we can improve our site and business to your satisfactionn. In that case, leave your comment on the site to help us meet your expectations.
How will my audience usually access my site
This site is made to be responsive and easily accessible, irrespective of the type of device you have to view, as far as it can browse be it mobile devices, laptop or deskktop.
